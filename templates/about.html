About Us

ARENT is a product of an idea flash, it is a free platform for users to find accommodation in a particular area on a map, the available rooms will appear as markers on the map. When the marker is clicked, a tooltip appears, it contains a link to another page which will give full detail of the selected apartment.

The platform also offer users the ability to upload items for sell or for hire, which other interested users can contact the one who uploaded the item for negotiation.
